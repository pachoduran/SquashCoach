# Data Deletion Instructions - Squash Coach V3

## How to Delete Your Account and Data

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app:

### Steps:

1. **Open** Squash Coach V3
2. **Go to Settings** (gear icon)
3. **Scroll down** to the bottom of the page
4. **Tap "Delete Account"**
5. **Confirm** the deletion when prompted

### What Gets Deleted:

- Your user account and login credentials
- All match data stored in the cloud
- All player analysis data
- All shared match records

### Important Notes:

- This action is **irreversible** — once deleted, your data cannot be recovered
- Local data stored on your device will also be cleared
- If you have shared matches with other users, those shared records will also be removed

### Alternative: Delete Only Cloud Data

If you want to keep using the app but remove your cloud data:
1. You can choose not to sync matches to the cloud
2. Local data on your device will remain available

### Need Help?

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, or if you need assistance, please contact us at:
- **Email**: squashcoachapp@gmail.com

We will process your deletion request within **48 hours**.

### Data Retention

After account deletion:
- All personal data is permanently removed from our servers
- No backup copies of your data are retained
- The deletion is complete and final
