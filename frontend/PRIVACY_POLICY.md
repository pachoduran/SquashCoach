# Privacy Policy - Squash Coach V3

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Introduction

Squash Coach V3 ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application designed to help squash players track and analyze their matches. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (used for login and account identification)
- Username (chosen by you)

### Match Data
- Match scores, player names, and match statistics
- Match dates and game details
- Analysis data generated from your matches

### Technical Data
- Device type and operating system version
- App version

## How We Use Your Information

- **Account Management**: To create and maintain your account
- **Match Tracking**: To store and display your match history and statistics
- **Cloud Sync**: To synchronize your match data across devices (optional)
- **App Improvement**: To understand usage patterns and improve the app

## Data Storage

- Local match data is stored on your device using SQLite
- Cloud data is stored on our secure servers when you choose to sync
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your match data is private and only accessible by you.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data at any time through the app
- **Delete** your account and all associated data (Settings > Delete Account)
- **Export** your match data
- **Opt out** of cloud sync and keep data only on your device

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:
1. Open the app
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm the deletion

For more details, see our [Data Deletion Instructions](DATA_DELETION.md).

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
